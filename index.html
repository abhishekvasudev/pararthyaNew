Our Projects

SAVE OKHLA BIRD SANCTUARY

Save Okhla Bird Sanctuary - Pararthya is working closely with the conservation of Okhla Bird Sanctuary, a wetland heaven for over 300 bird species. It is one of the 466 IBAs (Important Bird Areas) in India. But now, there has been an observant decrease in population of migratory birds due to increase in air, noise and water pollution in the Yamuna River, further shrinking the habitat of marshlands and water areas.
